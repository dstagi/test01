Making Bread

  1. Get out the bread.
  2. Lay two slices down
  3. Get out the hummus
  4. Open it and spread it on one side of each slice of bread with a knife
  5. Thinly slice red bell peppers, cucumbers, and horseradish havarti
  6. Place a layer of each topping on one of the slices of bread
  7. Place the toppingless side of bread hummus side down on the topping covered bread
  8. Eat
